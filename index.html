Game Rules & Mechanics

  • Objective: Place letters strategically onto the Grid and Skip boards to optimize your score before ending the game.
  • Current Round Restrictions: You cannot press Next Round or End Game until all 5 tiles in the Current Round row have been cleared out and placed elsewhere.
  • Dynamic Wildcards: Wildcard slots unlock progressively based on your usage of rare Challenge Letters (K, J, Q, X, Z). For every challenge letter you clear out of its container row, exactly one wildcard slot becomes active.
  • Typing Wildcards: To assign a value to an unlocked wildcard, type a single character into the input box and hit Enter. It will occupy the first available active wildcard slot.
  • Row Multipliers: Clicking the gray multiplier box (×1) on the right side of any grid row will toggle its value between positive and negative numbers. Multipliers can still be altered after the game ends.
  • Scoring Structure:
    • Grid Column payouts scale up progressively from left to right: (0, 1, 2, 3, 5, 7, 10 points).
    • Skip slots apply incremental penalties: (0, 0, -1, -1, -2 points).
  • Round Commitments: Advancing the round locks all tiles present on the Grid, Skip, and Round rows, preventing them from being moved again.
